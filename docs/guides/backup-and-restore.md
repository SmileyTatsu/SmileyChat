# How to Backup and Restore Your Data

SmileyChat is a **local-first** application. All of your data—chats, characters, settings, and personas—is stored entirely on your computer in standard files. There are no hidden databases or cloud locks.

## The `userData` Folder
Everything that makes your SmileyChat *yours* lives in one folder:
`userData/`

Inside this folder, you will find:
- `characters/`: All your character cards and avatars.
- `chats/`: Your chat logs and uploaded images.
- `personas/`: Your user profiles and avatars.
- `settings/`: Your preferences, connection profiles, and encrypted API keys.
- `presets/`: Your custom AI generation settings.

## How to Backup
1. Fully close SmileyChat.
2. Copy the entire `userData` folder.
3. Paste it onto a USB drive, cloud storage, or another safe location.

## How to Restore or Move to a New PC
1. Install SmileyChat on the new computer.
2. Before starting it for the first time, replace the empty `userData` folder with your backed-up `userData` folder.
3. Start SmileyChat. All your chats, characters, and settings will be exactly as you left them.
